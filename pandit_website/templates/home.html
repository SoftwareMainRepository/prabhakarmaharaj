DIWALI POOJA

OFFICE POOJA

An office puja, often referred to as a workplace puja or office blessing ceremony, is a religious or spiritual ritual conducted in an office setting, primarily in countries with a strong cultural and religious influence, such as India.

Shanti Puja

Shanti Puja, also known as Peace Puja, is a Hindu religious ceremony performed to invoke peace, tranquility, and harmony in one's life, family, or community.

Sanskar Vidhi

"Sanskara" in Hinduism refers to a rite or ritual that marks significant life events and transitions. These rituals are considered essential for a person's spiritual and cultural development.

Havan/Yagna

A "Sanskar Hawan Yajna" is a sacred fire ritual in Hinduism that is performed during various life events and rites of passage to invoke divine blessings and purification.
